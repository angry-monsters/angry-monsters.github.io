  Show Formatting Help:  

These shortcuts can be used in stat and feature names and descriptions.

Use _ to italicize and ** to bold, ^^ for a bold italic.

[MON]: Shows the NPC's name.
[CHA]: Shows the NPC's charisma modifier.
[3D6]: Computes 3d6.
[STR ATK]: Calculates the modifier to the NPC's attack roll for a strength-based attack.
[DEX 2D8]: Calculates the damage roll for a dexterity-based attack with damage dice 2d8.
[WIS SAVE]: Calculates the save DC vs the NPC's wisdom.
[3D6 + 1], [STR ATK - 2], [WIS SAVE + 3]: Adds a modifier to the given values.
[DC]: Shows the save DC based on the NPC's tier.
[ATK]: Shows the attack bonus based on the NPC's tier.
